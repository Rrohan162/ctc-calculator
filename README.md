A comprehensive and user-friendly Salary CTC (Cost to Company) Calculator designed for the **New Tax Regime (FY 2025-26)**. This application helps users understand their salary structure, calculate in-hand pay, and visualize tax deductions.

## âœ¨ Features

- **Accurate Tax Calculation**: Built for the latest FY 2025-26 New Tax Regime slabs.
- **Detailed Breakdown**:
  - **Earnings**: Basic, HRA, Special Allowance, Bonus, etc.
  - **Deductions**: PF, Professional Tax, Gratuity, Income Tax.
  - **Net Pay**: Monthly and Yearly in-hand salary.
- **Smart Metrics**:
  - Visual breakdown of where your money goes (Tax vs. In-Hand vs. Bonus).
  - Detailed tooltips for complex components like Gratuity and PF.
- **Incentive & Bonus Handling**: Separate inputs for Performance Bonus and monthly incentives.
- **Home Loan Support**: Calculate tax exemptions based on Home Loan Interest and Rent Received.
- **Responsive Design**: Beautiful, glassmorphism-inspired UI that works on all devices.
